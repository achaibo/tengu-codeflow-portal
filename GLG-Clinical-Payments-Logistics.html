Executive Summary

A complex backdrop of multi-country payment rails, stringent KYC/AML/tax rules, FX volatility, approval bottlenecks, immense site workload, and fragile system integrations.

Unified Rails Greenphire for all payments and travel execution.

Operational Playbook

The End-to-End Workflow

This is the operational core. The process moves from event capture to rule application, execution, and final reconciliation.

1. Event Captured A visit is completed in CTMS, a form signed in EDC, or a drug dispensed in IRT.
2. Rules Engine Maps Logic The platform maps the event to payment and travel rules for all stakeholders.
3. Execution The transaction is executed (card load, ACH, booking) and notifications are sent.
4. Reconciliation & Audit An immutable, timestamped log is created for inspection and ERP posting.

Phase-Specific Operations

Phase I (The Celerion Lens)

  • Tempo: Intense, high-tempo, 24/7 ops.
  • Money: Same-day stipends, high screen-fail volume.
  • Setup: Instant disbursements, bundled triggers, site-side exception tools.

Phase II/III (The Global Oncology Lens)

  • Tempo: Massive scale, many sites, heavy travel.
  • Money: Complex flows (stipends, OOP, caregiver), high-volume site grants, FX/tax issues.
  • Setup: Full CTMS/EDC/IRT integration, travel orchestration, local carve-outs.

Global Operations Playbook (By Region)

A high-level summary of regional nuances for payment rails, KYC/tax, and transport.

North America: Card + ACH are standard. 1099 prep is key.
Europe/UK: Bank transfers common. GDPR and municipal transport mandates are critical.
LATAM: Bank transfers prevalent. Multilingual comms are essential.
APAC: Highly variable rails. Data residency checks required.
Middle East/Africa: Bank transfers primary. Visa/security steps must be built into the workflow.

Risk & Mitigation

Proactively identifying and mitigating common pain points is the key to smooth operations.

Pain Point Likely Root Causes Mitigations I Deploy
Transport Approval Delays Manual reviews; unclear caps; after-hours gaps. Pre-approved templates; auto-approve within caps; 24/7 escalation roster.
Over/Under-Payments Protocol ↔ CTA misaligned; edge cases untested. Detailed Trigger Matrix; rigorous regression testing; monthly recon.
Country Rule Friction Unclear KYC/tax rules; card restrictions; municipal mandates. Country Readiness Checklist; bank rails fallback; whitelisted local vendors.
Site Workload & Disputes Confusing portals; opaque status; invoice mismatches. Two-click site UX; transparent accruals; aligned CTA terms; QBRs on disputes.
Participant Churn Slow funds; unclear comms; caregiver costs. Near real-time payouts; multilingual comms; clear caregiver rules.
Inspection Risk Fragmented logs; manual overrides. Immutable unified logs; strict change control; quarterly QA reviews.
