My Strategic Framework

Phase 1: Proactive Start-Up & Site Activation

Flawless execution begins with meticulous planning.

  • ✓
    Strategic Site Onboarding: Move beyond simple protocol review. I conduct a "Day in the Life" simulation of the study workflow to identify and resolve operational gaps before the first patient is enrolled.
  • ✓
    Immersive Training: Deliver comprehensive training on the protocol, the science behind the therapy, and the specific patient profile to create site enthusiasm and ensure deep understanding.

Phase 2: Dynamic & Adaptive Monitoring

Maintain momentum through data-driven oversight.

  • ✓
    Hybrid Monitoring Approach: Combine centralized, remote monitoring of EDC data with purposeful on-site visits focused on high-value activities like targeted SDV and relationship building.
  • ✓
    Early Data Review: Work with data management to conduct frequent interim analyses to promptly identify recruitment bottlenecks, protocol deviations, or safety signals, enabling swift implementation of CAPA plans.

Phase 3: Sustained Engagement & Inspection Readiness

Finish strong by prioritizing quality and compliance.

  • ✓
    Patient-Centric Retention: Develop robust patient support systems and work with sites to minimize the logistical burden on participants, reducing dropout rates in long-term trials.
  • ✓
    Perpetual Inspection Readiness: Maintain an "always ready" TMF by ensuring contemporaneous document filing. I conduct internal "mock" audits to ensure the entire team is confident and prepared for regulatory scrutiny.
