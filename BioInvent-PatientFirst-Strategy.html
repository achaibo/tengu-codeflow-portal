A New Vision for BioInvent's Clinical Trials

The Promise in Your Pipeline 🚀

BioInvent’s science is truly pioneering. In your labs, you are crafting the future of cancer treatment—therapies that don't just fight a disease, but empower a patient's own body to heal. This is more than just medicine; it's a new chapter of hope for people facing the most difficult diagnoses of their lives.

But the journey from a brilliant discovery to a life-changing treatment is one of the most challenging in modern medicine. The greatest hurdle we face is not in the science, but in reaching the very people that science is meant for.

The Challenge and The Opportunity 🎯

🔍 The Challenge

Recruiting and retaining diverse patient populations remains a primary obstacle. Oncology trials historically struggle to include underrepresented communities, limiting the generalizability of data. Furthermore, the increasing adoption of decentralized trials (DCTs) requires sophisticated strategies to ensure equitable patient engagement, data integrity, and regulatory compliance across the globe.

✅ The Proposed Solution

Overcome hurdles by implementing a strategy centered on:

  • ✔AI-Driven Patient Recruitment: Leverage technology to pinpoint eligible patients and build trust.
  • ✔Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI): Build robust initiatives to ensure trials represent everyone.
  • ✔Seamless DCT Integration: Use technology to reduce the burden on participants.

📈 The Projected Outcome

This integration will lead to:

  • ✔More efficient, inclusive, and patient-centric clinical trials.
  • ✔Accelerated development timelines for key therapies.
  • ✔More robust, representative data to meet global patient needs.

Our Blueprint for Hope: A Patient-First Strategy 🗺️

❤️

AI with a Human Heart

Use AI to analyze real-world data to find individual patients, not just statistics, and connect them with the right trials faster.

🤝

Inclusion as Our Cornerstone

Build genuine partnerships with community doctors and organizations to ensure the future of medicine includes everyone.

🏠

Trials That Come to the Patient

Integrate user-friendly tech like eConsent, telemedicine, and wearables to reduce patient burden and improve engagement.

Bringing the Blueprint to Life for Your Pipeline 💡

🩸

For BI-1206 & BI-1607 (Blood Cancers & Melanoma)

Use AI to mine hematology-oncology registries. Partner with trusted foundations like the Lymphoma Research Foundation and Melanoma Research Alliance for outreach and provide proactive telehealth support.

🫁

For BI-1808 & BI-1910 (Solid Tumors)

Utilize AI to scan genomic databases for patients with high TNFR2 expression. Launch a community-centric model with oncology networks and ease participation with a DCT model using wearable tech.

🦠

For BT-001 (Oncolytic Virus)

After using AI to find patients meeting complex criteria, create a dedicated nurse navigator program to provide a direct human connection, guide patients, and ensure they feel valued.
