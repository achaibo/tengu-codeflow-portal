A High-Stakes Trial at a Crossroads

I was tasked with providing strategic oversight for a pivotal Phase III global trial evaluating a novel biologic therapy for non-small-cell lung cancer (NSCLC) and gastric cancer. This trial spanned over 200 sites... The situation demanded decisive, hands-on leadership to not only solve the immediate problems but to create a massive acceleration in study execution.

Strategic Actions & Solutions Implemented

I architected bespoke action plans for each country, addressing unique regulatory and recruitment barriers.

I established a daily, hands-on partnership with the CDx group to push through lab approval delays.

Optimized Patient Recruitment with Data-Driven Tools
