2. Getting Started with Your Custom GPT

Your Custom GPT lives within your ChatGPT environment. Accessing it is simple:

  1. Log In: Go to chat.openai.com and log in to your ChatGPT account.
  2. Locate Your GPT: On the left-hand sidebar, find the "GPTs" or "My GPTs" section. Your custom AI assistant will be listed there by name (e.g., "[Your Business Name] Business Assistant").
  3. Start Chatting: Click on its name to open the chat interface. Type your requests in the text box at the bottom and press Enter.

4. Practical Applications: Your GPT in Action

Your Custom GPT is built to supercharge your business. Here are just a few ways to use it.

I. Operational Efficiency & Admin Support

  • Drafting Communications: Example: "Draft an email to the team announcing our new remote work policy."
  • Summarizing Information: Example: "Summarize this meeting transcript and identify the action items."
  • Scheduling & Reminders: Example: "Draft a reminder email for John's appointment tomorrow at 10 AM."

II. Sales & Lead Acceleration (Internal Use)

  • Drafting Follow-Ups: Example: "Write a polite follow-up email to a prospect who was interested in our [Product Name]."
  • Summarizing Interactions: Example: "Summarize my call notes with ABC Corp, focusing on budget, timeline, and pain points."
  • Brainstorming Angles: Example: "My client is hesitant about the price. Give me 3 ways to reframe the value to justify the cost."

III. Marketing & Content Creation

  • Social Media Content: Example: "Write 5 Instagram caption ideas for our new spring collection."
  • Product Descriptions: Example: "Create a compelling product description for our new organic coffee blend."
  • Blog & Ad Ideas: Example: "Generate 3 short headlines for a Facebook ad promoting our consulting services."
