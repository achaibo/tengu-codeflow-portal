3. Data Strategy

  • Platform: REDCap Cloud; 1 dynamic visit form.
  • Source: ePRO = source; site eCRFs limited to AE verification & bottle counts.
  • QC: Auto range & missing edits; weekly query cycle; lock after SAE recon.
  • Archive: REDCap “Offline ZIP” + SHA‑256 hash stored in TMF.

5. IP Supply & Accountability

  • GMP lot COA, HDPE 120‑caps bottles, ambient FedEx 2‑Day with TempTale.
  • Site storage 15‑25 °C; daily min/max; bottle IDs logged in REDCap.
  • Returns reconciled at Week 12; site destroys or ships to depot.

7. Statistical Analysis Plan Summary

  • Primary Analysis: Paired t‑test; confirm normality (Shapiro).
  • Secondary: LMM with fixed effects = visit; random intercept = participant.
  • Safety: AE counts by PT and severity.
  • Interim Data Cut (Week 20): Descriptive enrollment & adherence only.
